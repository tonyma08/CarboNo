About Us

Our Mission

Our goal is to assist you in tracking the amount of carbon emssions you produce in your everyday lives. By using our website, we hope you will be able to understand the composition of your daily carbon emission and how it compares to the average carbon emission of your region. By spreading more awareness, we hope to reduce humanity's carbon footprint, leading to a society that says NO to carbon -- CarboNo!

Who are we?

The CarboNo Team: We are a group of computer science students from UC Santa Cruz, who are also interested in taking care of our planet. We believe that knowing the degree of damage each one of us causes to our planet helps raise our awareness.

This Project

This project was created during CruzHacks 2020, a hackathon located in Santa Cruz, California, USA between 1/17/2020 and 1/19/2020. To see the creation process, source files, and collaborators, visit our Github repository for this project.
