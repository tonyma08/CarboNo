Our Mission

Our goal is to assist you on tracking the amount of carbon emssion you produce in everyday lives.
Through the usage of our website, you will be able to understand the composition of your daily carbon emission, how comparing to the average carbon emission of your region.

Who are we?

The CarboNo Team: We are a group of computer science students from UC Santa Cruz, who are also interested in taking care of our planet.
We believe that knowing the degree of damage each one of us cause on our planet help raise our self-consciousness.
